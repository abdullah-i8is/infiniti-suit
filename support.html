Support Policy

Support Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Infiniti Suite's Support Policy page. Our goal is to provide you with the best possible support experience. This policy outlines the scope, availability, and terms of our support services.

Scope of Support

Covered Services: Our support services cover technical assistance with the installation, configuration, and usage of Infiniti Suite products. We also provide troubleshooting for issues and bugs.

Excluded Services: Our support does not include assistance with third-party products, custom development, or issues arising from modifications made to our software by unauthorized parties.

Support Channels

Email Support: You can reach our support team via email at support@infinitisuite.com. We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.

Phone Support: Phone support is available at +1-800-123-4567 during our business hours, Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM (EST).

Online Resources: Our website offers a comprehensive knowledge base, FAQs, and user guides to help you find quick answers to common issues.

Response Times

Our response times are based on the severity of the issue:

  • Critical Issues: Response within 1 hour. Includes major system outages or severe bugs affecting all users.
  • High Priority Issues: Response within 4 hours. Includes significant functionality problems impacting multiple users.
  • Medium Priority Issues: Response within 8 hours. Includes isolated bugs or issues affecting non-critical functions.
  • Low Priority Issues: Response within 24 hours. Includes general questions, minor issues, or feature requests.

Support Availability

Our support team is available during the following hours:

Monday to Friday: 9 AM to 5 PM (EST)

We are closed on weekends and public holidays. Any inquiries received outside of our business hours will be addressed on the next business day.

Escalation Process

If you are not satisfied with the resolution of your support request, you can escalate the issue by contacting our support manager at supportmanager@infinitisuite.com. Please include your original ticket number and a detailed description of your concerns.

Feedback

Your feedback is valuable to us. After your support request is resolved, you may receive a survey asking for your feedback on the support experience. We use this information to continually improve our services.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact our support team:

Infiniti Suite Support Team
Email: support@infinitisuite.com

Thank you for choosing Infiniti Suite. We are committed to providing you with exceptional support.
